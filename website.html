About Travis Scott:

Travis Scott, originally Jacques Webster II, is a Houston-born rapper, singer, and producer known for his distinctive blend of hip-hop, trap, and psychedelic music. His albums like "Astroworld" and "Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight" have received critical acclaim for their innovative production and infectious hooks. Scott's career has been marked by collaborations with artists across genres, solidifying his position as a trendsetter in modern music.
